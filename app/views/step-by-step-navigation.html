Get information from the Ministry of Justice: step by step

Check what type of information request to make to the Ministry of Justice (MOJ) and how to make it.

  1. Step 1 Check what type of information request to make

    The type of information request depends on the information you want.

    1. Check what type of request to make
    2. Your rights under the Data Protection Act 2018

  2. Step 2 Make an information request to the MOJ

    1. Make a Freedom of Infomation request (FOI)
    2. Request personal information for you, someone you know, or someone you're representing (Subject Access Request - SAR)
    3. Make an Environmental Impact request (EIR)

  3. Step 3 After you've made a request

    You should expect a response within 30 days of making your request.

  4. Step 4 If you want to complain

    If you're not happy with the response to a request, or you think the MOJ has misused information, you can complain to the Data Protection Officer.

    email: privacy@justice.gov.uk
