Check which type of information request to make

Use this service to check which type of information request to make to the Ministry of Justice (MOJ). The type of request you'll make depends on the information you want.

You can request information from:

  • Ministry of Justice
  • HM Prison and Probation Service (HMPPS)
  • HM Courts and Tribunals Service (HMCTS)
  • Legal Aid Agency (LAA)
  • Office of the Public Guaridian (OPG)

Checking involves answering up to 8 questions.
